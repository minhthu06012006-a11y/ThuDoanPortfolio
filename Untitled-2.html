NOCTOPUS

Bedtime Companion for Kids

Inspiration Behind the Project

NOCTOPUS emerged from a university programming course focused on family-oriented products with positive impact, particularly for children.

  • Encourages healthy sleep routines through gentle reminders.
  • Soft purple palette and cartoon characters for a relaxing vibe.
  • Combines UI/UX with front-end dev for meaningful family values.

Design Philosophy

User-centered approach prioritizes safety and delight, with features for parent-child interaction.

  • Lullabies and stories to ease bedtime transitions.
  • Animated octopus mascot builds emotional connections.
  • Simple navigation ensures accessibility for all ages.

Future Outlook

Testing showed improved routines; future: customizable reminders and more content.

  • Aims to nurture positive habits in young users.
  • Potential expansions: Mobile app integration.

Project Overview

Final university project: A website promoting timely bedtime for kids, blending education and fun. Tools: HTML/CSS/JS. Timeline: Course duration.

Problem Statement

Children often resist bedtime; need engaging tools for positive routines.

User Research

Surveys with 20 parents/kids: 80% struggle with sleep schedules. Personas: 'Timmy' (kid 5-10) needs fun; 'Parent Sarah' seeks engagement.
