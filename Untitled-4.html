The Challenge

Problem Statement

Despite being one of Vietnam's most significant national holidays, Hung Kings' Commemoration Day (Ngày Giỗ Tổ Hùng Vương) lacks a cohesive digital presence. Information is scattered across multiple sources, often presented in outdated formats that fail to engage younger generations.

The challenge was to create a platform that not only consolidates accurate historical and cultural information but also presents it in a way that resonates with modern users while maintaining cultural authenticity and respect.

Our Approach

Solution

We designed and developed a comprehensive website that serves as both an educational resource and an interactive platform for cultural engagement. The solution bridges traditional values with modern web technologies to create an immersive experience.

By combining rich visual storytelling, intuitive navigation, and community-driven features, we created a digital space where users can learn about, participate in, and contribute to the preservation of this important cultural celebration.

Key Features & Design Decisions

Cultural Visual Identity

Designed with traditional Vietnamese aesthetics in mind, featuring warm earth tones, refined decorative patterns inspired by ancient temples, and carefully selected imagery that evokes cultural familiarity and respect.

Intuitive Information Architecture

Structured content into clear sections: Homepage, Tour Schedule, Festival Activities, Community Contributions, and Donation Portal. Each section is designed for easy navigation and progressive disclosure of information.

Interactive Timeline

A visual timeline showcasing the history of Hung Kings and the evolution of the commemoration day, allowing users to explore historical events through an engaging, scroll-based narrative.

Community Engagement

Integrated donation system with QR code support and real-time "Top Donors" leaderboard to encourage community participation and promote transparency in cultural preservation efforts.

Responsive Design

Fully responsive layout optimized for all devices, ensuring consistent user experience from mobile phones to desktop computers. Special attention to touch interactions and mobile-first navigation patterns.

Accessibility First

Designed with WCAG 2.1 AA standards in mind, featuring proper color contrast ratios, semantic HTML structure, keyboard navigation support, and screen reader compatibility to ensure inclusivity.

Design Process

01

Research & Discovery

Conducted user interviews with 25+ participants across different age groups to understand their knowledge and perception of Hung Kings' Day. Analyzed existing cultural websites and identified gaps in digital representation.

02

Information Architecture

Created site maps and user flows based on research insights. Organized content into logical hierarchies that balance educational depth with ease of navigation. Conducted card sorting exercises to validate structure.

03

Visual Design

Developed mood boards inspired by Vietnamese traditional architecture and art. Created a design system with culturally appropriate color palettes, typography, and visual elements. Iterated through multiple design concepts with stakeholder feedback.

04

Prototyping & Testing

Built interactive prototypes in Figma and conducted usability testing with 15 users. Identified pain points in navigation and information comprehension. Refined interactions based on user feedback.

05

Development & Launch

Implemented responsive front-end with semantic HTML, modern CSS, and vanilla JavaScript. Ensured cross-browser compatibility and optimized performance. Conducted final QA testing before competition submission.

Reflection

Key Learnings

  • Cultural Sensitivity: Learned the importance of balancing modern design trends with cultural authenticity. Every design decision was validated against cultural appropriateness.
  • User-Centered Design: Discovered that different age groups interact with cultural content differently. Younger users preferred interactive elements while older users valued detailed historical information.
  • Technical Constraints: Learned to optimize performance while maintaining rich visual experiences. Implemented lazy loading and image optimization techniques.
Future Improvements

Next Steps

  • Multilingual Support: Add English and other language options to reach international audiences interested in Vietnamese culture.
  • Virtual Tour: Implement 360° virtual tours of Hung Kings Temple using WebGL technology for immersive experiences.
  • Mobile App: Develop a companion mobile application with offline access to content and location-based features for visitors.
