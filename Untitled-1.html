Esports Hub

A website for Esport lovers

Inspiration Behind the Project

Born from a passion for gaming, Esports Hub tackles the fragmented esports landscape where fans juggle platforms like Twitch for streams and ESPN for news.

  • Unified experience for millions of esports viewers.
  • Cyberpunk aesthetics to capture high-stakes gaming energy.
  • Developed over 2 weeks in Figma for a UX/UI course.

Design Philosophy

Centered on user empathy, with research showing 75% of gamers want seamless access. Features like personalized feeds foster community.

  • Game-specific streaming views for titles like Valorant or League of Legends.
  • Responsive layouts prioritizing mobile (60% of viewership).
  • Interactive overlays for stats and chat, inspired by pro broadcasts.

Future Outlook

Prototype metrics: 40% faster tasks, 90% satisfaction. Reflections emphasize iterative feedback and WCAG-compliant neon designs.

  • Potential additions: Social sharing and AR merch previews.
  • Aims to boost fan loyalty in the growing esports industry.
