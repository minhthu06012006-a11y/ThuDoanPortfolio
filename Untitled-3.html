TẾT Ở TRONG TA

An honor to a special holiday

Inspiration Behind the Project

Final Arttech Theory assignment: Develop an interactive exhibition concept, poster, visual identity, and supporting website inspired by bánh chưng and Tết traditions.

  • Blends technology with cultural storytelling.
  • Evokes warmth and nostalgia through modern design.
  • Team collaboration for multi-sensory experience.

Design Philosophy

User-centered, accessible interface embodying "Tết within us," guiding visitors through bánh chưng process.

  • Interactive homepage simulates unwrapping bánh chưng.
  • 3D visuals for immersive exploration.
  • Community gallery for shared moments.

Future Outlook

Successful university project; potential for real exhibitions. Emphasizes honoring Vietnamese heritage.

  • Expansions: AR features, multilingual support.
  • Aims to educate and ignite cultural pride.

Project Overview

University final: Interactive website for Tết-themed exhibition, blending art and tech. Tools: Figma, HTML/CSS. Timeline: Course end.

Need digital platform to showcase exhibition concept and guide visitors.
